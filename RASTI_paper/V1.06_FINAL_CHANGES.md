### ✅ m2. Figure Placement [t] Tag Issue - FIXED
**Problem**: [t] placement specifier appearing as literal text in PDF on pages 4, 7, 8, 10

### ✅ p5. Duplicate Citation Formatting - FIXED
**Problem**: Citations appearing as "Spirtes et al. 2000)Spirtes et al. (2000)"

**Root Cause**: Hardcoded citation text before \cite{} commands:
- `(Spirtes et al.~2000)\cite{spirtes2000}` was producing both the hardcoded text AND the natbib citation

**Solution**: Removed all hardcoded citation prefixes, letting natbib handle formatting:
- Fixed 11 instances of doubled citations
- Examples corrected:
  - `Spirtes et al.~2000)\cite{spirtes2000}` → `\cite{spirtes2000}`
  - `Kass \& Raftery~1995)\cite{kass1995}` → `\cite{kass1995}`
  - `André et al.~2010)\cite{andre2010}` → `\cite{andre2010}`
  - And 8 more similar fixes
